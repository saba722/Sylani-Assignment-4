Welcome
Ruediger Zarnekow


Dear students,
Welcome to the MOOC "Introduction to Information Systems". I am very pleased that you are interested in this course and in business informatics. In 25 video units and a series of accompanying texts, we have prepared the basic content of business informatics in an understandable form for you with a lot of effort. We placed great value on not only dealing with traditional content, but above all with current topics such as cloud computing, agile software development or digital business models. In times of increasing digitalization of our economy, from Industry 4.0, Big Data or Electronic Business, business informatics play a central role as a link between technological development and successful entrepreneurial application. I am trying to convey the understanding necessary for this in this MOOC. The structure of the course is chosen so that you can learn at your own pace without any time constraints. For each learning unit you will find learning goals that will bring you closer to the most important results of the learning unit in advance. Each chapter ends with a short quiz that you can use to check your learning progress and prepare yourself optimally for the final exam. In-depth discussion questions enable you to deepen individual focus areas depending on your interests. In the forum you can exchange ideas with other course participants as you wish. I hope you enjoy working on the course and hope that you, like the many students at TU Berlin, can benefit from the knowledge you have gained.
Best regards,
Prof. Dr. Rudiger Zarnekow
